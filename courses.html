B.Ed. admissions are based strictly on the guidelines of the TNTEU. Both male and female candidates with marks given in the tabular column below for UG degrees (including B.E. & B.Tech) are eligible to be admitted to the B.Ed. two-year degree programme, regarding Commerce and Economics candidate, a pass in the PG degree is mandatory.

M.Ed. admissions are based strictly on the guidelines of the TNTEU. Both male and female candidates with marks given in the tabular column above mentioned criteria in the UG/PG Degree (including B.E. & B.Tech) with B.Ed. degrees are eligible for admission to the M.Ed. two-year programme.
